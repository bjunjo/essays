Second Chance

by Robert T. Kiyosaki

My note

Seeing money as a tool to solve problems rather than just a paycheck resonates deeply with how I think about capital allocation and financial sovereignty. Kiyosaki’s take on “good debt” and embracing humility in learning feels like a reminder that long-term freedom comes from playing the right game thoughtfully, not blindly chasing security. It’s a solid nudge to keep growing as a generalist investor, especially when the paradigm is shifting beneath us.
