Moving to Portugal Made Simple

by James Cave

My note

Thinking about how Portugal balances affordability, safety, and microclimates reminds me how important it is to align environment with long-term well-being—something I’m always weighing in capital allocation and life decisions. This book’s honest take on the nuances of place and culture feels like a solid guide for anyone seeking more sovereignty over where and how they live. Imagine us, figuring out our own little corner of the world with that mindset.
