A Man for All Markets

by Edward O. Thorp

My note

Ed Thorp’s approach to figuring things out by experimenting and trusting numbers over noise really resonates with how I think about capital allocation and Bitcoin — it’s about carving your own path with clarity and patience. His focus on surviving and thriving through calculated, independent moves reminds me why sovereignty and long-term perspective matter more than chasing quick wins. Thought you’d appreciate how he blends math, mindset, and self-reliance in a way that feels both practical and quietly revolutionary.
