The Lessons of History

by Will Durant and Ariel Durant

My note

History doesn’t just repeat—it cycles through power, belief, and survival in ways that remind me how fragile our systems really are. Seeing how religion, economics, and governance rise and fall over centuries makes me think about why financial sovereignty and long-term thinking aren’t just practical—they’re necessary for navigating these slow-moving shifts. It’s a good lens for us to keep questioning what really lasts when everything else decays.
