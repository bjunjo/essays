Time Off

by John Fitch, Max Frenzel, and Mariya Suzuki

My note

Taking deliberate time off isn’t just about rest—it’s a strategic move in the long game of creativity and capital allocation. This book’s emphasis on focused work balanced with genuine incubation resonates deeply with how I think about letting ideas and value compound quietly over time. Feels like a reminder that sometimes, stepping back is the smartest way to move forward.
