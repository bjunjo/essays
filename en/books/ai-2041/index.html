Ai 2041

My note

AI’s single-minded pursuit of profit makes me think about the importance of aligning incentives with true user sovereignty—something I’m always wrestling with in capital allocation and Bitcoin’s promise. The way Kai-Fu Lee and Chen Qiufan explore ethical guardrails feels like a necessary compass for navigating a future where technology shapes not just markets but our very freedoms. It’s a reminder that long-term thinking means building systems that protect us from the short-term traps AI so easily creates.
