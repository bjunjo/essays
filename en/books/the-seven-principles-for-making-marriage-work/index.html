The Seven Principles for Making Marriage Work

by John Gottman and Nan Silver

My note

Building a strong marriage feels a lot like smart capital allocation — it’s about consistently investing time and trust in the relationship’s long-term health, even when the returns aren’t immediate or obvious. Prioritizing friendship and emotional attunement reminds me that the most valuable assets aren’t just financial, but the deep connections that sustain us through every market cycle of life. Here’s to growing that resilience together.
