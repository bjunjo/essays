Triumph of the City

by Edward Glaeser

My note

Cities are like living networks of opportunity, where density isn’t just about buildings but about the flow of ideas and capital—something I see mirrored in Bitcoin’s decentralized promise. Thinking long-term, Glaeser’s view reminds me that true sustainability comes from embracing complexity and connection, not retreating into isolation. It’s a perspective that feels as important for how we build cities as for how we allocate capital and shape our own freedom.
