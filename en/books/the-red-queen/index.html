The Red Queen

by Matt Ridley

My note

Evolution’s relentless arms race reminds me how progress often comes from competing forces rather than cooperation—a bit like how Bitcoin thrives on individual incentives aligning without central control. It’s a sharp lens on long-term survival, where those who adapt best, not just those who play nice, shape the future—something that resonates deeply with how I think about capital and sovereignty.
