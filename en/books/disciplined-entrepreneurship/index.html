Disciplined Entrepreneurship

by Bill Aulet

My note

Focusing your capital and energy on a tightly defined market feels like the kind of disciplined move that builds real financial sovereignty over time. This book’s emphasis on testing assumptions and listening closely to customers reminds me how important it is to stay humble and adaptable—even when chasing big paradigm shifts. Hope it sparks some ideas for your own journey.
