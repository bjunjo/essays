My note

Learning to sit through the uncomfortable parts of investing—without chasing every shiny idea—is a skill this book sharpens in a way that feels really aligned with how I think about capital and time. It reminds me that true financial sovereignty isn’t about complexity or speed, but about mastering a few core principles and letting them play out patiently, just like holding Bitcoin through all the noise.
