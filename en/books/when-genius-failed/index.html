When Genius Failed

by Roger Lowenstein

My note

Watching Long-Term Capital’s rise and fall in *When Genius Failed* reminds me how even the smartest trades can crumble without humility and an eye on the bigger picture. It’s a sharp lesson in why true financial sovereignty means owning your risks, not just chasing convergence or short-term arbitrage — something I keep close when thinking about Bitcoin’s long game.
