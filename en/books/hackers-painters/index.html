Hackers & Painters

by Paul Graham

My note

Seeing how Paul Graham connects creativity with long-term value reminds me why I’m drawn to Bitcoin’s disruptive edge—both demand doing the hard, odd work that others avoid. It’s a sharp reminder that real progress comes from figuring things out as you go, not from following the average path or chasing quick wins.
