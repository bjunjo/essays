Reminiscences of a Stock Operator and the Investment Strategies of Jesse Livermore

by Edwin Lefèvre and Richard Wyckoff

My note

Watching Jesse Livermore’s discipline in waiting for the right play reminds me why patience is everything—whether allocating capital or holding Bitcoin through volatility. It’s a sharp reminder that the market doesn’t owe you a paycheck, and true edge comes from knowing when to act, not just acting for action’s sake. Hope you find the same clarity in his lone-hand approach as I do.
