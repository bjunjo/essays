No Two Alike

by Judith Rich Harris

My note

Understanding that personality differences come more from genes than environment reshapes how I think about long-term growth—not as something molded purely by external forces, but as a deep interplay with who we inherently are. It’s a reminder that true financial sovereignty and thoughtful capital allocation start with accepting what’s immutable inside us while navigating the world’s unpredictable shifts. Hope this perspective resonates with you the way it did for me.
