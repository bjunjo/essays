Range

by David Epstein

My note

Seeing how life rewards those who adapt and experiment, not just those who sprint ahead early, this book reminded me why I value mental flexibility in both investing and living. It’s like holding Bitcoin—embracing uncertainty, learning as you go, and knowing that the best moves come from a broad view, not just narrow specialization. Thought you’d appreciate this perspective as much as I do.
