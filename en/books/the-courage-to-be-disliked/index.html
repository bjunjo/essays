The Courage to Be Disliked

by Ichiro Kishimi, Fumitake Koga

My note

Freedom often feels like a high-stakes capital allocation—choosing where to place your trust and energy, knowing you’ll be disliked by some no matter what. This book’s reminder that being true to yourself means accepting that cost resonates deeply with how I think about sovereignty and long-term value, both in life and in Bitcoin. It’s a steadying philosophy for anyone learning to act small while thinking big.
