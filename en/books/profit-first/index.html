Profit First

by Mike Michalowicz

My note

Profit First feels like a practical blueprint for financial sovereignty—forcing discipline through structure rather than willpower alone. It’s a reminder that real freedom comes from setting boundaries around your money, much like how I think about capital allocation and long-term value. This approach turns budgeting into an act of self-respect, which feels aligned with the mindset I want us to build together.
