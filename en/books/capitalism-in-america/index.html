Capitalism in America

by Alan Greenspan, Adrian Wooldridge

My note

Watching how America harnessed steel and railways to reshape its destiny reminds me that real wealth comes from building lasting infrastructure—not just physical, but also conceptual. It’s a powerful reminder that paradigm shifts aren’t accidental; they require vision, patience, and a willingness to move capital where it can truly transform society. This perspective keeps me grounded in why I believe in Bitcoin and long-term sovereignty.
