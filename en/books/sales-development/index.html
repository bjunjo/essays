Sales Development

by Cory Bray, Hilmon Sorey, Ryan Reisert

My note

Seeing sales as a system rather than just hustle resonates with how I think about capital allocation—discipline and process bring clarity to uncertainty. This book’s focus on understanding where you are in the buyer’s journey reminds me how important it is to meet people (or opportunities) where they truly are, not where you wish they’d be. It’s a solid reminder that even in something as human as sales, long-term thinking and structure create the foundation for real sovereignty.
