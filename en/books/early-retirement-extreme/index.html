Early Retirement Extreme

by Jacob Lund Fisker

My note

Mastering a handful of skills well enough to navigate life’s complexities feels like the foundation of true freedom—something this book captures without the usual hustle mindset. It’s a reminder that building competence across areas, rather than chasing expertise or status, aligns with how I think about long-term sovereignty and capital allocation. Feels like a practical philosophy for living deliberately, not just accumulating.
