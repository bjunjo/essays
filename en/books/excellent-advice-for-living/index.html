MY NOTE

This book matters because it challenges the relentless hustle mindset that traps most capital allocators and thinkers alike. Embracing deliberate rest, selective ownership, and curiosity over certainty aligns directly with the long-term discipline Bitcoin demands—freedom comes not from endless growth, but from mastering the systems that sustain you. It’s a reminder that true wealth is measured in time and values, not just money or opinions.
