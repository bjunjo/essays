Quantitative Momentum

by Wesley R. Gray, Jack R. Vogel

My note

Momentum investing feels like a natural complement to the patient, long-term capital allocation I believe in—it’s about recognizing patterns without chasing hype. This book’s focus on disciplined, data-driven stock selection resonates with my approach to building sovereignty through thoughtful, incremental moves. I think you’ll appreciate how it bridges the gap between abstract strategy and practical action, much like how I view Bitcoin.
