If It's Raining in Brazil, Buy Starbucks

by Peter Navarro

My note

Seeing how different economic schools clash and complement each other in Navarro’s book reminded me how critical it is to understand the underlying narratives driving markets, especially when allocating capital for the long haul. It’s a sharp reminder that true financial sovereignty comes from anticipating shifts, not just reacting to headlines—something I think about every time I revisit Bitcoin’s paradigm-breaking story. Hope you find the macro plays as fascinating as I did.
