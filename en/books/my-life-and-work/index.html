My Life and Work

by Henry Ford

My note

Ford’s methodical approach to innovation reminds me that real progress comes from small, deliberate experiments, not sudden leaps—a mindset that feels crucial when navigating paradigm shifts like Bitcoin. His focus on efficiency and reducing unnecessary friction parallels how I think about capital allocation: clear, intentional moves that build long-term sovereignty. Reading this with you makes me appreciate how patience and precision can reshape entire industries, one step at a time.
