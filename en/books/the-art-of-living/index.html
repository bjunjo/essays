The Art of Living

by William Hart

My note

True freedom starts in the mind, and *The Art of Living* reminded me how much our reactions shape not just our suffering but our future. It’s a powerful lens for someone like me, chasing long-term sovereignty—not just over capital, but over thoughts and actions too. Thought you’d appreciate how it echoes that balance between patience and agency we’re always talking about.
