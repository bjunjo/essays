The New American Century

by George Friedman

My note

Seeing how the American Republic was consciously invented as a regime built on invention and struggle reminds me how important it is to shape the systems we live in rather than just inherit them. It’s a reminder to think long-term about sovereignty—not just personal, but collective—and how that shapes identity and power over time. Makes me appreciate why holding Bitcoin feels like holding a piece of that ongoing experiment.
