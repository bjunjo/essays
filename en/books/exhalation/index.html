Exhalation

by Ted Chiang

My note

Reading *Exhalation* felt like a reminder that growth—whether of minds, relationships, or even capital—demands patient, intentional care from others, not just isolated effort. It resonates with how I see sovereignty: true freedom comes with responsibility and deep connection, not detachment. Thought you’d appreciate its quiet insistence that long-term thinking isn’t just about strategy, but about nurturing what really matters.
