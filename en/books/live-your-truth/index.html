Live Your Truth

by Kamal Ravikant

My note

Living your truth feels a lot like the kind of capital allocation I believe in—investing deeply in what matters most, even when the payoff isn’t immediate or guaranteed. Kamal’s reminder that our inner world shapes the quality of everything outside resonates with the long game I’m playing, both in life and with Bitcoin. this one feels like a nudge to keep leaning into what’s real and letting go of the noise.
