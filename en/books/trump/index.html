Trump

by Donald J. Trump, Meredith McIver

My note

Managing capital, like managing a business or even your own time, demands that same fierce attention to detail and willingness to course-correct before problems spiral. Trump’s emphasis on treating company money as your own really resonates with how I think about deploying capital—long-term, intentional, and with a mindset of sovereignty. It’s a reminder that momentum isn’t given; it’s earned through vigilance and small, deliberate actions.
