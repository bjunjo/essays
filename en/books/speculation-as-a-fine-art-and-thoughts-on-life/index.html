Speculation as a Fine Art and Thoughts on Life

by Dickson G. Watts

My note

True wisdom lies in having the courage to rethink what we hold dear, and Watts reminds me that capital allocation isn’t just about numbers—it’s about patience, humility, and knowing when to fold. It’s a quiet call to focus on principles over noise, much like holding Bitcoin through the storms, trusting that real value reveals itself in time.
