My note

Sometimes the hardest part isn’t finding the right tools or skills, but understanding the system you’re working within—and that’s what Garone really nails. It reminded me how long-term mastery and flow come from not just showing up, but refining your process with humility and intention, much like capital allocation where every move matters over time. Thought you’d appreciate how this mindset applies beyond work, even in the quiet discipline of self-development.
