My note

Seeing how The Blockchain Group blends innovative financial tools with a steadfast Bitcoin accumulation strategy really resonates with my belief in long-term capital allocation and financial sovereignty. Hope this gives you a fresh perspective on how the future of finance might unfold.

TBG's long-term vision extends beyond just accumulating Bitcoin. The company aims to be a central player in the digital financial markets, integrating Bitcoin into a new financial architecture.

TBG is the first publicly listed Bitcoin Treasury Company in Europe, positioning it to capitalize on the European investment landscape and regulatory environment, including tax-advantaged investment wrappers where direct Bitcoin exposure may be limited

While MicroStrategy is the pioneer and leader in the Bitcoin Treasury Company model, and Metaplanet has seen significant success in Japan, The Blockchain Group distinguishes itself through its innovative approach, particularly with Bitcoin-denominated convertible debt.

The free allocation of share subscription warrants (BSA) to TBG's existing shareholders serves two main purposes: to reward shareholder loyalty by providing them with a potentially valuable asset that can be traded on the market, and to accelerate TBG's Bitcoin accumulation strategy.

Bitcoin Yield," along with "BTC Gain" and "BTC € Gain," are Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) adopted by The Blockchain Group to measure the success of its Bitcoin Treasury Company strategy.

TBG has innovated in this area by issuing convertible debt that can be subscribed to and is denominated in Bitcoin, aligning investor incentives with the company's Bitcoin-centric strategy.

TBG's goal is to enhance shareholder value by increasing the amount of Bitcoin per share, leveraging Bitcoin's potential appreciation.

The Blockchain Group (TBG) is Europe's first Bitcoin Treasury Company, listed on Euronext Growth Paris. TBG's core strategy, launched in November 2024, involves the long-term accumulation of Bitcoin through strategic capital raises, including innovative methods like Bitcoin-denominated convertible debt.
