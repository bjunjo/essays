Men Are From Mars, Women Are From Venus

by John Gray

My note

Recognizing that men and women communicate and process emotions so differently feels like understanding two distinct ecosystems—each with its own logic and currency. This book reminds me how important it is to honor those differences, not to fix or change, but to build connection and respect, much like how thoughtful capital allocation respects unique assets for long-term growth. It’s a useful framework for us to navigate complexity together, appreciating not just outcomes but the journey of understanding each other.
