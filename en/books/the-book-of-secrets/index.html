The Book of Secrets

by Osho

My note

Breathing is the one constant in a life defined by change, much like how true financial sovereignty comes from embracing uncertainty without clinging to outcomes. This book reminds me that the mind’s endless seeking can pull us away from the present—the real place where freedom and value exist, whether in self or capital. For you, Elsa, I think it’s a gentle nudge to lean into openness and trust that some things unfold without force, just like the best investments.

My highlights

56 highlights from Kindle. These are the lines I stopped at.

Your life is your breath.

between these two points of birth and death you must breathe.

We are breathing continuously from the moment of birth to the moment of death. Everything changes between these two points. Everything changes, nothing remains the same, only breathing is a constant thing between birth and death.

The mind has to be turned from seeking to non-seeking.

The moment you start seeking you have moved away from the present, away from yourself, because you are always in the present.

you cannot seek truth. You can find it, but you cannot seek it. The very seeking is the hindrance.

The truth is in the present, and mind is always in the future or in the past, so there is no meeting between mind and truth.

Mind is a mechanism of desiring. Mind is always in desire, always seeking something, asking for something. Always the object is in the future; mind is not concerned with the present at all.

Really, freedom is freedom from you.

we cannot ask how to surrender. It happens! Love happens, surrender happens.

Love is being totally open, vulnerable.

Once you know the trick how to love, then love will not flower because you can create a facade, a deception.

It is not renunciation; there is no fight involved at all.

Once you know something you become free from it.

Remember, it is ultimately your mind which makes anything attractive or unattractive. It is you who is the deciding factor.

The more you try to leave it, the more attractive it becomes. The more you want to negate it, the more you feel invited.

unless this sex orientation is dissolved, man cannot be natural, normal.

Madness is so normal that not to be mad may look abnormal.

The danger is not in Tantra; it is in you. And the appeal of yoga is because of you, because of your ordinary mind, your sex-suppressed, sex-starved, sex-indulging mind.

Tantra says be aware of the desire; do not create any fight. Move in desire with full consciousness, and when you move into desire with full consciousness you transcend it. You are into it and still you are not in it. You pass through it, but you remain an outsider.

Yoga is suppression with awareness; Tantra is indulgence with awareness.

On the path of Tantra one does not have to fight at all. Rather, on the contrary, one has to indulge – but with awareness.

Yoga is not philosophy, just like Tantra – yoga depends on action, method, technique. Doing leads to being in yoga also, but the process is different. In yoga one has to fight; it is the path of the warrior.

If the technique is not for you, then nothing happens.

while you are playing your mind is more open. While you are serious your mind is not so open; it is closed.

Something may fit you. If it fits you, then be serious, and then go deep into it – intensely, honestly, with all your energy, with all your mind. But before that just play.

I say playing because you should not be too serious. Just play!

So first change the mind, then change from mind to no-mind.

If you have a mind, you go on creating a world; you go on imposing, projecting.

The world you are seeing, you are seeing because of a particular mind.

once the mind is different your character will be different.

Anger is just showing what is within.

Tantra says, you cannot change man unless you give him authentic techniques with which to change.

Choose a technique which fits you, put your total energy into it, and you will not be the same again.

Your religion is a coincidence, so do not cling to it. Use some scientific methods to transform yourself.

your beliefs are irrelevant. It is just a coincidence that you are born somewhere and someone else is born somewhere else. This is just a coincidence. Your religion is a coincidence, so do not cling to it. Use some scientific methods to transform yourself.

You are ignorant, you are in conflict, you are asleep. This is a disease, a spiritual disease.

do not create the fallacy that an argumentative mind is an alert mind. It is not, because the moment you move into arguments you have lost the awareness, you have lost the alertness.

Use it as an instrument to understand something, but do not go on creating barriers with it.

use your intellect only as an instrument, not as a master.

We will try to comprehend first intellectually. But use your intellect only as an instrument, not as a master.

mind is the doubt! Unless the mind dissolves, doubts cannot be cleared.

Mind IS the doubt. It is not that the mind doubts, mind is the doubt! Unless the mind dissolves, doubts cannot be cleared.

question. It is a machine to grind out, to create questions. So give it anything and it will cut it into pieces and create many questions.

That is the very nature of the mind – to question.

When you enter into love, into a deep, intimate world of love, the person disappears, the form disappears, and the lover becomes just a door to the universe.

We know ourselves only through others, and the others can only know the outer form. That is why we become identified with it.

Love can see a person as the person can see himself from within. Then the form disappears.

Why does this happen? This happens because really, everyone is formless. You are not a body. You move as a body, you live as a body, but you are not a body.

When love reaches its peak the lover disappears.

Tantra signifies through it a very meaningful thing: that the deeper teachings cannot be given unless there is love between the two – the disciple and the master.

For Tantra, doing is knowing, and there is no other knowing.

I have seen many people trying directly – reaching nowhere, because they are so full of garbage that first it has to be emptied out.

If it starts working, then forget everything, other techniques. Go on working on it. It does not matter how many techniques you try. What matters is that you try one technique to its very end, to its ultimate depth. And if you succeed in one technique, then every other technique becomes very easy.

And the easiest way is just to go through, read them, and any technique that suddenly strikes you “This is it!” – give it a try, at least for twenty-one days.

“This is what will be suitable to me” – try it.
