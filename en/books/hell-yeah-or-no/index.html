MY NOTE

This book cuts through the noise by focusing on clear, actionable choices that push you toward growth instead of safety—a mindset essential for anyone managing capital in uncertain systems like Bitcoin. Embracing what scares you isn’t just about courage; it’s about calibrating your decisions to long-term freedom and self-sovereignty. Hell Yeah or No reminds me that meaningful progress comes from small, deliberate moves aligned with what truly excites and challenges me right now.
