The Hidden Reality

by Brian Greene

My note

Thinking about multiple universes stretching endlessly like parallel investments reminds me how much our reality is just one slice of possibility—kind of like how capital allocation is about choosing which future to back, knowing there’s no guarantee. It’s humbling and inspiring to consider that, just as with Bitcoin, the biggest shifts happen when we embrace uncertainty and play the long game across dimensions we barely understand.
