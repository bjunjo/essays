The Money Men

by H. W. Brands

My note

Hamilton’s vision of banking as a tool to stabilize and grow the economy feels like an early attempt at shaping financial sovereignty—though obviously flawed by the power dynamics of his time. It’s a reminder that true capital allocation isn’t just about money moving around, but about who controls the levers and how long they hold them. Reading it, I kept thinking about how Bitcoin flips that script, putting power back where it belongs: with the individual.
