The Communist Manifesto

by Karl Marx

My note

History’s always been about who controls the means of production and who’s left out, which makes Marx’s take feel like a lens for understanding the power dynamics behind everything I’m watching unfold today. It’s a reminder that true sovereignty — whether financial or personal — means questioning not just the system we inherit, but how we choose to allocate capital and influence long-term change.
