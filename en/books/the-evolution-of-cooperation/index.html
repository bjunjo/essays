The Evolution of Cooperation

by Robert Axelrod

My note

Cooperation isn’t just idealism—it’s a strategy that emerges naturally when self-interest plays out over time without a central enforcer. Reading Axelrod reminded me how trust and reciprocity, like Bitcoin’s open network, can align incentives and build something stronger than any single player alone. It’s a quiet optimism about human nature that feels surprisingly relevant when thinking about long-term capital and personal sovereignty.
