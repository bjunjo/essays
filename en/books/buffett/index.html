Buffett

by Roger Lowenstein

My note

Watching Warren’s early obsession with value and discipline reminds me how much true capital allocation is about temperament, not just numbers. It’s a quiet kind of conviction—trusting facts over the crowd, staying patient when the world feels irrational—that feels aligned with the long game I’m drawn to in Bitcoin and beyond.
