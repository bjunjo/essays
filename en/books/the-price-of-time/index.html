The Price of Time

by Edward Chancellor

My note

Understanding that interest is really the price we pay for time reshaped how I see capital allocation—not just as numbers, but as a reflection of our patience and belief in the future. It’s a reminder that true financial sovereignty comes from respecting time’s value and choosing when to act small and when to think ₿ig.

Interest – the time value of money – lies at the heart of valuation.
