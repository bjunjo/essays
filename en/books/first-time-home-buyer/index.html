My note

Thinking about homeownership through the lens of capital allocation really shifts the game—this book breaks down how to turn your first place into a long-term asset that can generate real cash flow, not just a liability. It’s a solid reminder that financial sovereignty isn’t just about what you hold but how you put your money to work, even in real estate. Definitely worth keeping in mind as we plan our next move together.
