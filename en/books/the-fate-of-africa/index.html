The Fate of Africa

by Martin Meredith

My note

Reading about how arbitrary borders and brutal conquests reshaped entire continents reminds me why sovereignty—whether for nations or individuals—is something I hold sacred. The long shadows of those decisions show how short-term power grabs can fracture generations, a lesson that echoes deeply when thinking about capital and freedom. Hope this gives you a new lens on resilience and the cost of imposed order.
