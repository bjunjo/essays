The Great CEO Within

by Matt Mochary, Alex MacCaw, and Misha Talavera

My note

Clear agreements and thoughtful decision-making feel like quiet revolutions in a world obsessed with speed and ego. This book’s focus on taking full responsibility and cultivating self-awareness resonates with how I think about long-term sovereignty—not just over capital, but over how we show up for ourselves and each other. It’s a reminder that real leverage starts with discipline and humility, both inside and out.
