The First 90 Days

by Michael D. Watkins

My note

Starting any new chapter feels a lot like navigating uncharted territory—this book lays out a clear path to align expectations and secure early wins, which resonates with how I think about capital allocation: understand the landscape deeply before making moves. It’s a reminder that long-term success hinges on honest conversations and shared understanding, not just bold bets. Thought it might speak to you as much as it did to me.
