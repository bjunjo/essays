Founders at Work

by Jessica Livingston

My note

Seeing how some of the biggest breakthroughs came from people just solving their own problems reminds me why I value long-term thinking and owning your own path. It’s a good reminder that real innovation isn’t about forcing change but about patiently building something that fits into the way people already live—kind of like how I view capital allocation and Bitcoin’s quiet revolution.
