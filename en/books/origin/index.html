Origin

by Dan Brown

My note

Seeing how codes shape our understanding of meaning and truth in *Origin* reminds me how carefully we must choose what we invest in—whether ideas, capital, or beliefs. It’s a sharp reminder that real change comes from creating new frameworks, not clinging to old patterns, something that resonates deeply with how I approach sovereignty and long-term thinking. Thought you’d appreciate how it challenges us to shift perspectives, just like we do navigating this crazy world together.
