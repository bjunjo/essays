Zorba the Greek

by Nikos Kazantzakis

My note

There’s something raw and honest in Zorba’s embrace of life’s chaos that reminds me why financial sovereignty isn’t just about numbers—it’s about owning your choices, passions, and the freedom to live on your terms. It’s a reminder that long-term thinking isn’t just strategy, but a practice of dancing with uncertainty, one small, deliberate step at a time.
