The Big Score

by Michael S. Malone

My note

Watching how early Silicon Valley pioneers built not just companies but an entire ecosystem reminds me how true innovation demands patience and resilience—qualities that resonate with how I think about capital allocation and Bitcoin’s slow, steady revolution. It’s a reminder that paradigm shifts aren’t just about flashy breakthroughs, but the infrastructure and community quietly evolving in the background.
