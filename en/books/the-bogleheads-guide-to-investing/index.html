The Bogleheads' Guide to Investing

by Taylor Larimore, Mel Lindauer, Michael LeBoeuf

My note

Sticking to a simple, disciplined plan feels like the quiet power move in a world obsessed with quick wins and hype. This book’s emphasis on rebalancing and owning the whole market resonates with how I think about controlling risk and preserving sovereignty over my capital. It’s a reminder that steady, patient moves often beat chasing the next big thing.
