Kettlebell Simple & Sinister

by Pavel Tsatsouline

My note

Building strength and mobility with simple, consistent movements feels like a small but powerful way to invest in myself—kind of like how I think about capital allocation. Pavel’s approach reminds me that mastering fundamentals over time creates real freedom, whether in body or mind, and that steady discipline compounds in ways that matter. Hope this helps you feel more grounded and ready for whatever’s next.
