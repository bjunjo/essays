Wisdom of Wealth

by Poornesh K.K.

My note

There’s a quiet power in how Poornesh weaves timeless wisdom with practical capital insights—reminding me that true financial sovereignty isn’t just about the returns, but the patience and discipline behind them. This book echoes the long game I believe in: thinking ₿ig while acting deliberately, turning capital into freedom and purpose. It’s a steady compass for anyone navigating the noise of quick wins.
