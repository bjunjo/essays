The Mystery of Capital

by Hernando de Soto

My note

Understanding where real wealth begins—far upstream in legal property systems—reshapes how I see capital allocation beyond just numbers on a screen. It reminds me that true financial sovereignty depends not only on what we hold, like Bitcoin, but on the invisible frameworks that give those assets life and meaning over the long run.
