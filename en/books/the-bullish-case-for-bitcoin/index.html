The Bullish Case for Bitcoin

by Vijay Boyapati and Michael Saylor

My note

Understanding Bitcoin as a new form of “high-powered money” reshapes how I see capital allocation—not just chasing short-term gains, but anchoring value in something scarce and sovereign. This book helped me connect the dots between long-term financial sovereignty and the philosophical shift needed to embrace a truly decentralized monetary base. It’s a reminder that real value isn’t handed down by governments, but emerges from trustless, game-theoretic systems we choose to believe in.
