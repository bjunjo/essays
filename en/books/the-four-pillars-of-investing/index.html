My note

Investing feels less like a sprint and more like tending a garden, and Bernstein’s breakdown of the four pillars reminds me why patience and discipline matter so much—especially when rebalancing nudges me to buy low and sell high, even if it means swimming against the tide. It’s a solid framework for building financial sovereignty, something I know you appreciate as much as I do.
