Flashpoints

by George Friedman

My note

History’s power shifts remind me how fragile empires are when they lose sight of long-term resilience—something Bitcoin teaches us differently. Friedman’s view on civilizations’ rise and fall feels like a call to build systems rooted not in conquest or control, but in true sovereignty and trust over time.
