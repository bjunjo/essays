Seven and a Half Lessons About the Brain

by Lisa Feldman Barrett

My note

Brains, like markets, thrive on flexible connections and constant rewiring—this book reminded me how plasticity is the ultimate form of resilience, whether in neurons or capital. Thinking about our own mental “airport hubs” makes me appreciate how delicate yet powerful our internal networks are, much like the ecosystems I trust to allocate capital long-term.
