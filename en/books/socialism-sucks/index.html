Socialism Sucks

by Robert Lawson and Benjamin Powell

My note

There’s a quiet urgency in understanding why systems that promise equality so often settle for sameness—and how that undercuts real progress and incentive. This book lays bare how central planning steals not just wealth but the future itself, which resonates deeply with why I hold Bitcoin and believe in financial sovereignty. Thought you’d appreciate its clear-eyed take on why freedom, at its core, demands private property and long-term thinking.
