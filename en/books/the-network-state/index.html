The Network State

by Balaji Srinivasan

My note

Building something new means rethinking not just systems, but the stories and values that hold them together. Balaji’s vision of a network state feels like the natural next step for sovereignty—where capital, culture, and community align beyond borders, all rooted in shared truth and long-term thinking. It’s a reminder that real innovation starts with a clear moral compass, not just technology.
