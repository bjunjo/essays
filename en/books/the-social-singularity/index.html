The Social Singularity

by Max Borders

My note

The idea that technology can reshape how we organize society without relying on old power structures really resonates with me. It feels like a roadmap for new paradigms—ones that align with financial sovereignty and long-term thinking, where we’re not just passive observers but active creators of the future. I see this as a fresh lens on how we might allocate not just capital, but trust and collaboration, in a rapidly changing world.
