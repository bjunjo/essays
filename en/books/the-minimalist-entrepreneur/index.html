The Minimalist Entrepreneur

by Sahil Lavingia

My note

The way Sahil breaks down building something small but meaningful really resonates with how I think about capital allocation—lean, iterative, and anchored in genuine passion. It’s a reminder that sovereignty starts with owning your process and learning fast, not waiting for permission or perfect conditions. Feels like a mindset that fits both entrepreneurship and long-term Bitcoin thinking.
