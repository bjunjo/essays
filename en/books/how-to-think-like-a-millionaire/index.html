How to Think Like a Millionaire

by Marc Fisher, Marc Allen

My note

Clear vision isn’t just a business tool—it’s the foundation of everything I believe about long-term thinking and sovereignty. This book reminded me how much our beliefs shape not only our financial outcomes but the very way we see possibility, something that feels especially important when building a life that’s truly our own. It’s a good nudge to keep cultivating that boldness, even when the path isn’t obvious.
