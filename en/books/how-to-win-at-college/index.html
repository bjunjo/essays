How to Win at College

by Cal Newport

My note

Balancing ambition with the need to recharge feels like a rare skill, especially when long-term thinking pulls you forward relentlessly. Newport’s push to intentionally stretch your talents while carving out space for genuine fun resonates — it’s a reminder that building financial and personal sovereignty isn’t just about grinding, but also about pacing yourself wisely. This one’s for you to keep pushing limits without losing the joy in the process.
