Layered Money

by Nik Bhatia

My note

Seeing how the Bank of Amsterdam shaped modern money by layering trust and liquidity makes me appreciate Bitcoin’s radical break from that old monopoly. It’s a reminder that true financial sovereignty comes from reclaiming that first layer, not just navigating the second. This book deepened my long-term view on how money evolves—and why real change takes patience and conviction.
