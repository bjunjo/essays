Apocalypse Never

by Michael Shellenberger

My note

It’s refreshing to see a clear-eyed take on climate fears that cuts through the noise without denying the facts. Shellenberger’s perspective reminds me how important it is to focus on adaptable solutions and long-term resilience—values that resonate with how I think about capital and sovereignty. Figured you’d appreciate this grounded view amid all the hype.
