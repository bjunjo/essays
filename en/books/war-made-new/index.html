War Made New

by Max Boot

My note

Seeing how power and trade have always been intertwined, Max Boot’s take on the Gunpowder Age reminds me that real sovereignty comes from mastering both strategy and resources over the long haul. It’s a sharp reminder that even empires rose and fell based on how well they adapted to paradigm shifts—something I try to keep front and center when thinking about capital and Bitcoin’s role in reshaping our future.
