The Way to Love

by Anthony De Mello

My note

Learning to let go feels like the core of both love and freedom—something this book captures in a way that resonates with how I think about capital and life. Clinging only breeds fear and rigidity, while true sovereignty comes from embracing change and flowing with it, whether in relationships or in the markets. This reminded me how much courage it takes to unlearn and open up, not just to others but to ourselves.
