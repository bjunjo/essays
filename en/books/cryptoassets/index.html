Cryptoassets

by Chris Burniske and Jack Tatar

My note

Understanding how to securely acquire and store cryptoassets feels like building the foundation of true financial sovereignty—something this book lays out with practical clarity. It’s a reminder that long-term capital allocation isn’t just about picking the right asset, but also about choosing the right infrastructure to protect and grow it over time. Thought you’d appreciate how it ties together mindset and technical diligence in this evolving space.
