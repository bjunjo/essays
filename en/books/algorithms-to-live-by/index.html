Algorithms to Live By

by Brian Christian, Tom Griffiths

My note

Life often feels like balancing between order and chaos, and this book nails how algorithms help us navigate that tension—reminding me that sometimes a little mess is actually the smartest move. It’s a solid lens for thinking about capital allocation and long-term strategy, where perfect info rarely exists but good enough decisions compound over time. Feels like a quiet nod to embracing uncertainty while staying deliberate—something I know you’ll appreciate as much as I do.
