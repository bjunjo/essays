Influence, New and Expanded

by Robert B. Cialdini

My note

Understanding the subtle power of reciprocity feels a bit like navigating the ripple effects of long-term capital allocation—small gestures can build trust or create unseen obligations over time. This book made me think about how freedom, whether financial or personal, often depends on recognizing when to give, when to hold back, and how customized value creates genuine connections without strings attached. Hope it sparks some thoughts for you, Elsa, about balance in relationships and in life.
