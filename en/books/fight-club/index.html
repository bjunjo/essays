Fight Club

by Chuck Palahniuk

My note

There’s something raw and honest about how *Fight Club* calls out the traps we build around ourselves—whether it’s chasing status, stuff, or false identities. It reminds me that true freedom, like financial sovereignty, comes from knowing what you really want and having the courage to break the cycle, even if it means collecting scars along the way. Thought you’d appreciate its brutal kind of clarity.
