Sold Out

by James Rickards

My note

Supply chains are the unseen arteries of the global economy, and *Sold Out* lays bare how fragile they really are—especially when so much depends on just a few key players like TSMC and Samsung. It’s a sharp reminder that true financial sovereignty means understanding these hidden vulnerabilities and thinking long-term about where and how value is created and preserved. Watching these slow-moving shifts unfold feels like a real-world lesson in patience and strategic capital allocation.
