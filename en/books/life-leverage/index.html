Life Leverage

by Rob Moore

My note

The way Rob breaks down life into assets you can leverage — people, ideas, capital — really resonates with how I think about allocating my own time and resources. It’s a sharp reminder that managing life isn’t about squeezing hours, but about aligning long-term vision with daily choices, something that feels deeply connected to financial sovereignty and thinking ₿ig. Hope it sparks some fresh ways to invest your most precious capital: time.

Life management, previously known as time management, is about leveraging other time-related assets such as people, capital, ideas, information, software, systems

Discipline isn’t about pain, self-negation or sacrifice as much as it is about having a vision: a longer-term view of who and where you want to be,

You can only ‘manage’ time by managing your life and how you invest the time that you have, which is equal to the time everyone has.

Time is being invested or wasted. There is no ‘in between’.

Time is our most valuable and precious commodity, slowly running out, slowly catching up on us.

If you don’t do something that’s important to you, or you can’t balance all the things in your life, then you aren’t managing your priorities effectively.

So it’s never about how much time you or anyone else has or hasn’t, and all about how you choose, use and invest that time, that makes all the difference – the difference between being somebody and being anybody.

This book isn’t called ‘Time Leverage’, it is called Life Leverage. It’s about leveraging elements of yourself and your life, to make it appear as if you have more time.

You don’t ‘manage’ time, you ‘manage’ yourself. You manage your life.

The more you think about ‘managing’ time, the more of a slave to it you become.

There’s no such thing as ‘time management’

Life Leverage: how to get more done in less time, outsource everything and create your ideal mobile lifestyle.

It is a life philosophy of getting more achieved in less time. Maximum leverage: minimum wastage.

You can start any business, anywhere in the world, from a small device you hold in your palm.

Life Leverage is living the things you are a master at, and outsourcing the things you are a disaster at. Growing in the areas of highest value to you, and letting go of everything else.

Life Leverage is about outsourcing all the things in your life that don’t make you feel alive.
