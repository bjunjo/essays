The Pocket Oracle and Art of Prudence

My note

Wisdom often hides in subtlety, and Gracián’s reminder to be prudently unpredictable resonates deeply when navigating markets and life alike. Holding steady in long-term vision means knowing when to reveal your hand and when to stay deliberately mysterious—something I find invaluable in both capital allocation and personal growth. This book feels like a quiet guide to mastering the art of moving thoughtfully through a world that rewards the patient and the perceptive.
