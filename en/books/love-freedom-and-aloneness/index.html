Love, Freedom, and Aloneness

by Osho

My note

True freedom in love feels a lot like the space Bitcoin gives me — a balance between connection and sovereignty. Learning to respect the rhythm of togetherness and solitude, like Osho describes, feels essential not just in relationships but in how I allocate my attention and capital over the long run. Maybe that’s the kind of love that builds resilience, both in life and in the markets.
