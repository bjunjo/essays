The Tax Bomb in Your Retirement Accounts

by Josh Scandlen

My note

Thinking about how to preserve capital while staying flexible really resonated with me here—taxes are the quiet risk no one wants to face, especially when passing wealth forward. Josh’s take on Roth IRAs as a strategic shield aligns with my focus on long-term sovereignty and making every move count, not just for today but for generations. It’s a reminder that sometimes the smartest allocation is about timing and control, not just growth.
