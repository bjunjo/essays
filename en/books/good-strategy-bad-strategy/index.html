Good Strategy Bad Strategy

by Richard Rumelt

My note

Cutting through noise to find what really matters in strategy feels a lot like navigating the crypto world—both demand clear-eyed focus on the true obstacles, not just shiny goals. Rumelt’s emphasis on facing challenges head-on and avoiding fluff resonates deeply with how I think about capital allocation and long-term sovereignty. It’s a reminder that real strategy is less about grand visions and more about practical moves that build lasting value.
