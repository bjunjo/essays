The Accidental Superpower

by Peter Zeihan

My note

Geography isn’t destiny, but it sure sets the stage—and Zeihan’s take on how America’s natural advantages shape its long game really resonated with me. Thinking about capital allocation, it’s clear that understanding the underlying “terrain” of geopolitics is just as crucial as spotting financial trends; it reminds me why sovereignty and long-term perspective can’t be separated from where and how we place our bets.
