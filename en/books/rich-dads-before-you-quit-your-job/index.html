Rich Dad's Before You Quit Your Job

by Robert T. Kiyosaki

My note

Success without steady vigilance feels a lot like flying blind — it’s easy to miss the cracks until the whole thing starts to fall apart. This book’s reminder to focus on the details and build durable foundations resonates with how I think about capital allocation and long-term sovereignty; it’s not just about spotting opportunities, but protecting the parachute before the jump. Thought you’d appreciate its honest take on the messy balance between ambition and patience.
