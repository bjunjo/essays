How to Win Friends and Influence People

by Dale Carnegie

My note

Appreciation feels like a quiet superpower, especially when building trust over the long game—whether in relationships or capital allocation. Noticing the good in others without slipping into empty flattery mirrors the patience and respect I try to bring to both Bitcoin and us. It’s a reminder that real connection, like true financial sovereignty, comes from understanding and valuing what’s beneath the surface.
