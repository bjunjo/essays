The Sales Development Playbook

by Trish Bertuzzi

My note

Seeing sales as a disciplined process that balances persistence with genuine service feels a lot like effective capital allocation—knowing where to focus energy and when to step back. This playbook reminded me that building pipeline, like building long-term value, demands patience, intentionality, and a clear strategy for what not to do. Thought you’d appreciate how it connects hustle with thoughtful, principled execution.
