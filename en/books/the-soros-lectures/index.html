The Soros Lectures

by George Soros

My note

Understanding that human thinking shapes outcomes as much as it tries to predict them resonates deeply with how I approach capital allocation and Bitcoin’s long game. Soros’s insight on testing ideas—not to confirm but to challenge—feels like a vital mindset for navigating financial sovereignty in a world full of shifting paradigms. Hope you find his blend of philosophy and practical skepticism as grounding as I do.
