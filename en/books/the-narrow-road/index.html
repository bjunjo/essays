The Narrow Road

by Felix Dennis

My note

Counting real wealth in cash you can touch feels brutally honest in a world full of illusions—something Felix Dennis nails in *The Narrow Road*. It reminds me that true financial sovereignty isn’t just about piling assets, but about owning freedom to act long term and give back on your own terms. This one’s for you —because building something lasting always means knowing when to hold on and when to let go.
