Way of the Wolf

by Jordan Belfort

My note

Trust isn’t just about what you sell, but the story behind it and the confidence you inspire—something that resonates deeply when thinking about Bitcoin’s long game and financial sovereignty. Belfort’s focus on emotional certainty reminds me how much of capital allocation is less about spreadsheets and more about connecting with conviction over time. Feels like a useful lens for both investing and building trust in an uncertain world, don’t you think?
