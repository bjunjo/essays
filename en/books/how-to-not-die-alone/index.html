How to Not Die Alone

by Logan Ury

My note

Learning to approach relationships like a long-term capital allocation makes so much sense after reading this—small, consistent efforts build clarity on what truly matters, just like assembling a resilient portfolio. It’s a reminder that waiting for perfect timing or the “ideal” partner often means missing out on valuable data from the reps we skip. Thinking about love through this lens feels like the kind of sovereignty I want to practice with everything I commit to, especially with you.
