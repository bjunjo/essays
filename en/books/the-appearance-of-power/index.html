The Appearance of Power

by Tanner Guzy

My note

How you show up visually can quietly amplify the value you already carry, much like how capital allocation magnifies returns over time. This book’s take on alignment between your wardrobe and your personal goals reminded me that every choice—whether in Bitcoin or button-downs—should serve a long-term vision of self-sovereignty and authenticity. Thought you’d appreciate how it blends practical style with deeper intention.

My highlights

10 highlights from Kindle. These are the lines I stopped at.

recognizing your own relationship with clothing is only half the battle.

He’ll wear a suit and tie when he needs to, but he doesn’t look great.

the manifestation of what is aesthetically pleasing isn’t limited to one particular clothing style.

It’s about being able to have the way you look enhance your life.

Appearance, unlike morality, isn’t about right or wrong, it’s about effective vs ineffective.

However, that magnification can be very real and have a dramatic impact on your life.

Your clothing and style won’t ever create value for you. The best they can do is magnify it.

The key to dressing well is establishing a wardrobe that is consistent with both your realities and your aspirations.

the purpose of this book is to outline the underlying principles of how clothing affects men and masculinity.

Regardless of what your intentions are, your clothing says something about you.
