The Five Dysfunctions of a Team

by Patrick M. Lencioni

My note

Trust feels like the foundation beneath any strong team—without it, even the best intentions fall apart. This book reminded me how much vulnerability and honest confrontation matter, whether you’re managing capital or navigating relationships. It’s a good nudge to build real connection beyond surface-level wins, something I think about a lot, especially with you.
