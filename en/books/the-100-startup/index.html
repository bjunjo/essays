The $100 Startup

by Chris Guillebeau

My note

Building something meaningful doesn’t require perfect timing or grand plans—just a clear offer that genuinely helps others and a simple way to get started. This book’s focus on practical steps and emotional value reminds me why real sovereignty begins with small, intentional actions that serve people over hype. It’s a solid reminder that long-term freedom grows from consistent, humble capital allocation—not chasing the next big thing.
