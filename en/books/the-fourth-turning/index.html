The Fourth Turning

by William Strauss and Neil Howe

My note

Cycles ripple through time in ways we rarely see coming, yet they quietly shape the world we inherit and pass on. This book reminds me that generational shifts aren’t just historical curiosities—they’re the undercurrents driving paradigm changes, much like Bitcoin’s slow but seismic reshaping of finance and sovereignty. Thinking long-term means tuning into these rhythms, even when the noise of the present tries to drown them out.
