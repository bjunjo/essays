The End of Jobs

by Taylor Pearson

My note

The way Taylor breaks down the death of gatekeepers and the rise of accessible entrepreneurship feels like a roadmap for anyone wanting to reclaim control over their time and capital. It’s a reminder that real freedom isn’t about playing it safe but about embracing the uncertainty and shaping your own path—something I think Bitcoin embodies perfectly. This one’s for us thinking big while acting small, building sovereignty step by step.
