Templeton Plan

by Sir John Templeton

My note

Humility and generosity aren’t just virtues here—they’re strategic moves in the long game of capital allocation and life. It reminds me that real success, whether in Bitcoin or business, comes from giving more than expected and learning from those who’ve walked the path before. This approach feels like the quiet backbone of lasting change, something I want us both to keep close.
