MY NOTE

This book matters because it cuts through the centralized chokeholds strangling innovation and self-sovereignty in tech and money. Gilder shows how open protocols and token economies—like Bitcoin and Ethereum—are not just financial experiments but necessary systems breakthroughs that reclaim freedom from monopolistic giants and regulatory overreach. Understanding this dynamic is crucial for anyone serious about allocating capital toward a truly decentralized future.
