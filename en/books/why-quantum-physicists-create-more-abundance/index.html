by Greg Kuhn

My note

Aligning beliefs with desires isn’t just wishful thinking—it’s foundational to how we shape reality, much like allocating capital toward long-term value in Bitcoin. This book deepened my sense that true financial sovereignty starts inside the mind, where shifting your expectations is the quiet catalyst for real-world abundance. Thoughtful change in belief feels like the most underrated form of strategy.

You desire more money, a loving relationship, a great job, a wonderful home, a fancy car, etc., right? Invest your time in changing your beliefs and you’ll find yourself attracting those very things.

To play alchemist with the world, which is your birthright, you must first change your beliefs.

The more you can embrace the new information, the more you will see your personal life changing for the better - and the less uncomfortable you will be with these new paradigms that will someday be old-hat for your children and grandchildren.
