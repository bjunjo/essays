The Paradox of Choice

by Barry Schwartz

My note

Too many choices often feel like noise, drowning out what really matters — a lesson I keep in mind when deciding where to allocate capital or how to steward my time. Limiting options isn’t about giving up freedom, but about reclaiming it, so that I can focus on the long game and the few things that truly align with my values. Thought you’d appreciate this reminder that sometimes less *is* more, especially when it comes to living intentionally.
