MY NOTE

This book challenges the very framework through which we seek freedom—showing that true liberation isn’t about accumulating knowledge or improving the self but about breaking free from all conditioning and identification. As a capital allocator who values sound systems and long-term sovereignty, I see Krishnamurti’s call to dissolve the “self” as a radical pivot from chasing progress to embracing presence—an essential mindset for anyone serious about freedom beyond currency or culture.
