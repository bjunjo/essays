A Little History of Philosophy

by Nigel Warburton

My note

Thinking about how philosophy insists we question everything, this book reminded me why long-term thinking and challenging dogma are so crucial—whether in capital allocation or life. It’s a practical nudge to live deliberately, stay skeptical, and focus on building a simple, meaningful path, much like the kind of financial and personal sovereignty we both strive for.
