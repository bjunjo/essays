by George Friedman

My note

Seeing the U.S. as an empire that shapes the world quietly but powerfully reminds me how influence often operates beneath the surface—much like how capital allocation works over time. It’s a good lens for thinking about long-term shifts and the subtle forces that define sovereignty, whether for nations or for us as individuals navigating systems bigger than ourselves.
