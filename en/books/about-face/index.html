About Face

by David H. Hackworth and Jocko Willink

My note

Discipline and clarity under pressure aren’t just battlefield virtues—they’re essential for anyone managing capital and navigating paradigm shifts. This book’s raw honesty about leadership and staying power reminded me how crucial it is to respect the fundamentals while keeping your eyes on the long game. Thought you’d appreciate its blend of grit and grace in the face of chaos.
