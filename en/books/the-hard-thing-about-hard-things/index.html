MY NOTE

This book cuts through the noise by insisting that clear, context-specific decisions trump generic models—an approach that mirrors Bitcoin’s emphasis on precise, trust-minimized systems over abstract theories. In capital allocation and leadership alike, you must act decisively with imperfect knowledge, embracing complexity rather than hiding behind expertise. That mindset—knowing what you want and owning the process—is how real freedom and long-term value emerge.
