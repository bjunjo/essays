The Fabric of Reality

by David Deutsch

My note

Reality feels less fixed after reading Deutsch—like certainty is always just out of reach, but that’s where real growth happens. It reminds me why long-term thinking and financial sovereignty matter: we don’t control the world, but we can navigate its complexity with humility and curiosity. Thought you’d appreciate how it blends philosophy and science in a way that echoes the mindset behind holding Bitcoin and capital allocation.
