The 8th Habit

by Stephen R. Covey

My note

Finding your voice feels a lot like discovering a rare asset—something uniquely yours that, when aligned with purpose, creates lasting value beyond just numbers. This book reminded me that true leadership and influence come from choice and authenticity, not titles, which resonates deeply with how I think about capital allocation and long-term impact. I hope it sparks something in you about owning your story and inspiring others along the way.
