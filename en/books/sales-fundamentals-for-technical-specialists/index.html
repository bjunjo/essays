by Janne Korhonen

My note

Understanding how to connect with someone beyond just the logic of a product feels like unlocking a new level in long-term relationship building, whether with customers or capital. Janne Korhonen’s take on sales as a thoughtful negotiation—where trust and shared values guide decisions—resonates with how I think about allocating capital and seeking financial sovereignty. It’s a reminder that even in technical worlds, humility and empathy can be the quiet drivers of meaningful progress.
