Interview Intervention

My note

Connecting through shared stories and genuine curiosity feels like the core of building trust—whether in an interview or in allocating capital over decades. Andrew’s approach reminds me that even in high-stakes moments, shrinking the world by finding common ground lets long-term relationships and opportunities take root naturally. It’s a simple but powerful way to practice sovereignty over how we present ourselves and the value we bring.
