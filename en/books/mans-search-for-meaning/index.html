Man's Search for Meaning

by Viktor E. Frankl

My note

Choosing your attitude feels like the ultimate form of sovereignty, especially when the external world seems designed to strip it away. Frankl’s reminder that even in the darkest moments, the freedom to decide how we respond is what defines us resonates deeply with how I think about capital allocation — patiently selecting where to place faith and resources amid uncertainty. It’s a powerful lens for long-term thinking, both in life and in Bitcoin.
