Direct Truth

by Kapil Gupta

My note

There’s something rare about a book that doesn’t just offer ideas but invites you to shed everything that’s not truly yours—like clearing the noise to focus on what really matters. “Direct Truth” feels like a blueprint for cultivating the kind of inner sovereignty that makes long-term thinking and wise capital allocation feel natural, not forced. Reading it, I kept thinking about how this kind of self-mastery is the ultimate foundation for freedom—something I hope we keep building together.
