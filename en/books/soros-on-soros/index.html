Soros on Soros

by George Soros

My note

Soros’s insistence on understanding *why* you’re losing money really hits home—it's a reminder that sticking to a long-term thesis means staying curious and humble, not stubborn. His blend of fierce focus and healthy insecurity feels oddly aligned with the mindset needed for true financial sovereignty and navigating paradigm shifts, much like Bitcoin’s own journey.
