Currency Wars

by James Rickards

My note

Watching how the Weimar hyperinflation empowered those who held hard assets and punished savers feels like a historical warning about the fragility of fiat money—and why true financial sovereignty matters. It’s a sharp reminder that capital allocation isn’t just about chasing returns, but preserving real value over time, especially when governments lose control. Thought you’d appreciate how this ties back to why I lean into Bitcoin and long-term thinking.
