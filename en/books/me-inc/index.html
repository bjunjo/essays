Me, Inc.

by Gene Simmons

My note

Owning your path means more than just chasing profits—it’s about being ruthless with your time and relationships to protect your vision. This book’s blunt reminder that you’re the CEO of Me, Inc. resonates with how I think about capital allocation and financial sovereignty: every decision is a deliberate step toward freedom, not just wealth. It’s a tough-love blueprint for building a life where long-term thinking isn’t optional, it’s mandatory.
