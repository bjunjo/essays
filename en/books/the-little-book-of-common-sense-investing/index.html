The Little Book of Common Sense Investing

by John C. Bogle

My Note

Bogle founded Vanguard and invented the index fund — and this book is the clearest explanation of why that matters. His core argument is simple: most active managers underperform the index over time, and fees compound against you just as returns do. What this book gave me was a long-term frame. Stop trying to beat the market. Own the market, keep costs low, and let time do the work. A must-read before you touch any investment.
