My note

Mastering public speaking feels a lot like capital allocation — it’s about assembling your thoughts with intention and conviction, then confidently putting them into action over time. This book reminded me that true confidence isn’t born from flawless phrases but from relentless practice and owning your voice, a mindset that’s as crucial in life and investing as it is on any stage.
