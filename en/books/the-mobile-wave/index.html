The Mobile Wave

by Michael J. Saylor

My note

Watching how each wave of technology reshapes our relationship with money and control makes me appreciate the mobile revolution as more than just gadgets—it’s a shift in how we allocate capital and claim sovereignty over our lives. Saylor’s perspective on the iPad as an experience, not just a device, reminds me that true innovation comes from empowering individuals to think and act differently, something I try to keep front of mind in both investing and living. This feels especially relevant now, as we navigate the next paradigm with Bitcoin and new forms of digital freedom.
