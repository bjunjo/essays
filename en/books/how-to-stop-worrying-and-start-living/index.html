How to Stop Worrying and Start Living

by Dale Carnegie

My note

Worry feels like a tax on the time and energy I want to dedicate to building long-term freedom, so this book’s reminder to focus on what’s right rather than what’s wrong really hits home. It’s a lesson in mental capital allocation—choosing where to invest your attention—and something I’m trying to practice every day, especially with everything uncertain in this world. Hopefully, it can be a gentle nudge for both of us to stop carrying unnecessary burdens and just live a little lighter.
