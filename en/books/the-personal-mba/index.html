The Personal MBA

by Josh Kaufman

My note

Building something meaningful means embracing the messy reality that perfection is impossible and small, consistent improvements add up over time. Kaufman’s focus on systems and honest measurement reminds me that whether it’s capital allocation or personal growth, long-term value comes from disciplined, thoughtful iteration—not shortcuts. For us, that mindset feels like a blueprint for financial sovereignty and freedom.
