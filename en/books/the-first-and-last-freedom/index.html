The First and Last Freedom

by Jiddu Krishnamurti

My note

Seeing the world clearly without the filters of belief or ideology feels like the foundation for true freedom—something I’ve come to value deeply in both capital and life. This book reminds me that understanding what *is*—without distortion or idealization—is the kind of clarity that underpins long-term thinking and real sovereignty, whether over money or the self. Thought you’d appreciate the way Krishnamurti cuts through the noise to the root of inner revolution.
