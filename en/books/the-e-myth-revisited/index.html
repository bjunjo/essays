The E-Myth Revisited

by Michael E. Gerber

My note

Seeing a business as a system designed around the customer’s experience really resonates with how I think about capital allocation—everything flows from a clear, long-term vision. It reminds me that success isn’t just about the assets you hold, but how thoughtfully you build and adapt the structures around them to create lasting value and sovereignty. This kind of mindset feels essential, whether you’re managing a portfolio or crafting a life.
