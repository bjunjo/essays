Make Money Trading Options

by Michael Sincere

My note

Watching markets shift requires patience more than prediction, and Sincere’s approach to probing small positions before committing feels like a practical reminder to respect volatility without rushing. It aligns with how I think about capital allocation—staying nimble, hedging thoughtfully, and always guarding sovereignty over my financial decisions. This book nudged me to appreciate options as a tool for measured engagement, not just quick wins.
