Becoming a Data Head

by Alex J. Gutman and Jordan Goldmeier

My note

Solving the right problem beats chasing the newest tech every time—this book reminded me to always anchor data work in clear value, not flashy tools. It’s a sharp lens for anyone who, like me, believes long-term gains come from thoughtful focus, not hype. I think you’ll appreciate how it cuts through noise to what really matters.
