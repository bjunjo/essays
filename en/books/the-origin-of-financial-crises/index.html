The Origin of Financial Crises

by George Cooper

My note

Seeing how traditional finance ignores the messy realities of human behavior and bank runs reminds me why I’m drawn to Bitcoin’s transparency and self-sovereignty. Cooper’s take on fat tails and feedback loops challenges the neat models we often trust, pushing me to think longer term and question what risk really means when the system itself can turn against you. Hope it sparks some of the same restless curiosity in you.
