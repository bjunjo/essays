Options for the Beginner and Beyond

by W. Edward Olmstead and Randell Olmstead

My note

Options are a different kind of game—like holding a key to potential without the full weight of ownership, which resonates with my view on capital allocation and sovereignty. This book helped me appreciate how timing and volatility shape outcomes, reminding me that patience and precision often beat impulsive moves—something that feels especially relevant when thinking about long-term bets like Bitcoin. Hope you find the nuances as intriguing as I did.
