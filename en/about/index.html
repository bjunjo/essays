About

I build, write, and invest.

In the beginning, my goal was simply freedom. Instead of trading my time to build someone else's dream, I needed to find assets that work while I sleep. In a world of expanding fiat, I needed a strong shield to protect the value of my labor, and I found that answer in Bitcoin.

But once I achieved a certain level of financial freedom, an unexpected emptiness set in. I kept asking myself: "What am I actually living for? In this one life I have, what am I going to contribute before I leave?" Investing solved the problem of survival, but it couldn't give me a purpose.

I may not be the genius inventor who creates from absolute zero, but I have a constant, undeniable urge to build. Adding positive-sum value to the world, creating tools that actually help people, and contributing to humanity in a meaningful way—that is what I truly want to do.

Right now, I am moving along two axes. On one hand, I allocate capital within the Bitcoin ecosystem (treasury strategies and early-stage startups). On the other, I am actively building and testing new ideas using AI agents. But if I am being completely honest, I still haven't found the single mission I want to dedicate the rest of my life to.

I am not someone who has it all figured out. I am still relentlessly searching for what I am uniquely good at and how I can best contribute. This space is a record of that search.

Why I write

To test my own thinking. Writing ruthlessly exposes the gap between what I actually know and what I just assume I know.

This site is an open notebook of how I make sense of the world. I leave it public hoping that people on a similar frequency might stumble across it. If something here resonates with you, reach out. I am looking for new perspectives, and I need your help as much as anyone else's.
