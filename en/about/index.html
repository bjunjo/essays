About

In short

Born in Busan, South Korea. Spent my teens and twenties in the San Francisco Bay Area. Studied cognitive science at UC Berkeley.

After graduating, I worked in Silicon Valley doing data analytics and business development. That's where I got pulled into investing.

Now I invest independently — focused on bitcoin, bitcoin treasury companies, and early-stage startups. I also invest through Antler Korea.

Seoul is my base, but I'm open to being anywhere.

What I think about

I'm less interested in investing itself and more interested in the moments when paradigms shift. Finding the people and companies moving in that direction is what I find genuinely exciting.

I think bitcoin is a signal that the monetary system itself is changing — not just another asset class. I could be wrong. But that's how I'm positioned.

Why I write

To think clearly. Writing forces me to find out what I actually know versus what I only think I know.

Keeping it public means that someday, someone thinking along the same lines might find it. No comments. If something resonates, reach out. I read everything.
