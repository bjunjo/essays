About

I read, write, and allocate capital.

For a while, I wanted to build something from scratch. But if I'm being honest with myself, I am not the inventor who creates from zero. I am an observer who spots massive shifts already in motion and places bets on them.

The goal was never just money. It was freedom. Instead of trading my time to build someone else's dream, I needed to find assets that work while I sleep. Books like The 4-Hour Workweek and The Almanack of Naval Ravikant pointed me in that direction.

We are living in an era where everyone has to survive on their own. In a world of expanding fiat, I needed a strong shield to protect the value of my time. I found that answer in Bitcoin. It's not just a trade; it's a matter of survival.

Today, I focus on watching the epicenters of paradigm shifts and allocating capital for the long term.

Why I write

To test my own thinking. Writing ruthlessly exposes the gap between what I actually know and what I just assume I know.

This site is an open notebook of how I make sense of the world. I leave it public hoping that people on a similar frequency might stumble across it. If something here resonates with you, reach out.
